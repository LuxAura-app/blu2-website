The Artist
Background

Mali V is a Brooklyn-born emcee operating at the intersection of raw street narrative and aspirational hunger. A member of the All Flights Delayed creative collective, he constructs vivid scenes from his New York City experience — unfiltered, uncompromising, and driven by a singular vision of ascension.

His music doesn't choose between the gritty and the glossy — it holds both in the same breath, the same bar, the same song. Whether tapping into introspective vulnerability on God Saves (Pain) or leaning into confident swagger on Ladies Love V, he delivers music that resonates because it is earned — lived, not imagined.
